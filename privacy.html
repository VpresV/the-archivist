Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

The Archivist is a free browser idle game available at thearchivistgame.net. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit and play the game, how it is used, and what choices you have.

By using this site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

The Archivist does not collect any personally identifiable information directly. However, third-party services used by this site may collect data as described below.

Your game progress — including knowledge count, upgrades, descent level, and achievements — is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with the game. Google Analytics may collect:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Referring website or search engine

This data is aggregated and anonymous. No personally identifiable information is collected. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Google AdSense

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to show relevant ads based on your browsing activity.

You can manage your ad personalization settings at adssettings.google.com. For more information, see Google's Advertising Policies.

AI-Generated Content

The Archivist uses the Anthropic Claude API to generate atmospheric lore fragments during gameplay. When a lore event triggers, a request is sent to a Cloudflare Worker proxy which forwards it to the Anthropic API. This request contains only gameplay context — milestone reached, upgrades owned, and game mode — and no personal information.

Cookies

The Archivist itself does not set cookies. Third-party services (Google Analytics, Google AdSense) may set cookies in your browser for the purposes described above. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through this site, please contact us.

Your Rights

Since The Archivist does not collect personal data directly, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete on our end. Your game save data is stored entirely in your own browser and can be deleted at any time using the "Reset all progress" option in the game.

For data collected by Google Analytics or AdSense, please refer to Google's privacy tools at myaccount.google.com.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through the Feedback form available in the game.

The archive keeps no records of those who pass through it.
